SECTION D - Real Diode Equation   (Q34 - Q36)
The i-v characteristics of the real diode in forward bias is governed by:
i = IS ( eV / nVT − 1 )
where IS = saturation current, v = diode voltage, n = ideality factor, VT = thermal constant.
